About e-SPACE ship

e-SPACE ship and our service such as Student

Espaceship is a brand new workspace in Mandaluyong City. Espaceship, as a new coworking cafe, our task is to provide peace and comfortability for freelancers, entrepreneurs, consultants, startups and students while they do their tasks and duties. Espaceship aims to be a well-known workspace cafe in the city that intends to satisfy customers with their provided services that includes free WiFi, food and beverages.

Our mission is to provide our customers with our consistent and great assistance whilst they get their homely stay in our workspace.
